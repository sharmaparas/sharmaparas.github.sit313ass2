Accommodation



  • If you preffer an individual place to yourself you can go to these two websites -
  • realestate.com.au is a rental website, where you can search for individual places.
  • Domain is also a rental website for individual places.
  • If you like sharing a place with new friends the following two options are for you -
  • Gumtree is a platform where you can find local advertisements for shared accomodations. or individual places as well.
  • Flatmates.com.au is another platform completely designed for people who would like to share a place.
